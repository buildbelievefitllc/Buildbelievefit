## TRACK 1: The Morning Primer
*Theme: Manifestation | Claiming the day | Leaving excuses behind*

### English

[Grounded opening breath]

You wake. The world is still. Before noise, before demand, before anyone else's voice... *this* is your sanctuary.

[Pause]

Today, you claim it. Not as hope. As *fact*. Your body knows what strength looks like. Your mind knows the path. Your excuses are not barriers—they are *fuel you choose to leave behind*.

[Pause]

This morning is the moment. Not tomorrow. Not after. *Now.*

[Breath]

One decision. One step. Everything changes because you decided it would.

[Final grounding breath]

This is your day.

## TRACK 2: The Reset
*Theme: 4-4-4-4 Box Breathing | Nervous system down-shift*

### English

[Slow, measured opening]

You are here. Your nervous system has been running. That ends now.

[Pause]

We breathe together. This is your reset.

[Beat]

Inhale for four. One... two... three... four.

[Pause]

Hold for four. One... two... three... four.

[Pause]

Exhale for four. One... two... three... four.

[Pause]

Hold for four. One... two... three... four.

[Beat - repeat]

Inhale for four. One... two... three... four.

Hold for four. One... two... three... four.

Exhale for four. One... two... three... four.

Hold for four. One... two... three... four.

[Final pause]

Your system is calming. You are safe. You are grounded.

[Closing breath]

Breathe.

## TRACK 3: The Anchor
*Theme: Child's Pose | Physical grounding | Mental release*

### English

[Grounded, intentional]

Come to the earth. This is not rest. This is *reclamation*.

[Pause]

Feet find the floor. Knees wide. Chest to your thighs. Forehead down.

[Breath]

You are held by the ground beneath you. The earth has your weight. Let it.

[Pause]

Here, there is no performance. No proof. No apology.

[Pause]

Your shoulders release. Your jaw softens. Your mind follows your body down.

[Deeper pause]

This small pose holds infinite power. Surrender is not weakness. It is *depth*.

[Breath]

You belong here. To this ground. To this moment. To yourself.

[Long pause]

When you rise, you rise *rooted*.

[Final breath]

Ground.

## NOTES FOR AUDIO PRODUCTION

- **Pacing:** Each track should be read with 1-2 second pauses at marked `[Pause]` and `[Pause longer]` points.
- **Vocal tone:** Deep, grounded, methodical. No rush. Authority without strain.
- **Breath markers:** Audible breathing at `[Opening breath]`, `[Breath]`, `[Final breath]` — these are production cues, not dialogue.
- **Box Breathing rhythm (Track 2):** The counting should be steady, with subtle vocal cadence. The four-count holds should feel spacious, not claustrophobic.
- **Silence:** Respect the silence. Silence is part of the sanctuary.
