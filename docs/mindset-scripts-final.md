## TRACK 2: The Reset
*Theme: 4-4-4-4 Box Breathing | Nervous system down-shift | Tactical recovery*

### English

[Slow, measured opening]
You are here. Your nervous system has been running. That ends now.
[Pause]
We breathe together. This is your reset.
[Beat]
Inhale for four. One... two... three... four.
[Pause]
Hold for four. One... two... three... four.
[Pause]
Exhale for four. One... two... three... four.
[Pause]
Hold for four. One... two... three... four.
[Beat - repeat]
Inhale for four. One... two... three... four.
Hold for four. One... two... three... four.
Exhale for four. One... two... three... four.
Hold for four. One... two... three... four.
[Final pause]
Your system is calming. You are safe. You are grounded.
[Closing breath]
Breathe.

## TRACK 3: The Anchor
*Theme: Tactical recovery | Physical grounding | Resetting the baseline*

### English

[Grounded, intentional]
Come to the mat. The physical toll is real.
[Pause]
When you are grinding through the day, the nervous system absorbs every ounce of that friction. This isn't about resting. This is tactical recovery.
[Pause]
Knees wide. Chest to your thighs. Forehead to the ground. Let the earth take the weight.
[Breath]
Your shoulders release. Your jaw softens. There is no performance here. No proof. Just resetting the baseline.
[Long pause]
Protect your peace. Protect your progress. When you rise, you rise ready.
[Final breath]

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Tone:** Raw, gritty, authoritative. No motivational fluff. Direct and tactical.
- **Pacing:** Measured and intentional. Respect the pauses — they are part of the power.
- **Breathing cues:** `[Breath]`, `[Grounded opening breath]`, `[Closing breath]` are production markers for audible breathing.
- **Box Breathing (Track 2):** Keep the four-count steady and spacious. The rhythm anchors the reset.
- **Silence is tactical:** Don't rush through pauses.
