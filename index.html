The architect

I built BBF because nothing fit the trenches.

I'm Akeem — architect of Build Believe Fit. I worked 12-hour shifts in medical manufacturing, then tried to train on everyone else's standard programs. None of them fit.

For seven years I ran the same failed experiment: clock out of a shift with wrecked legs and depleted CNS, then try to follow a program written for someone with eight hours of sleep and zero back fatigue. The templates assumed I was fresh. The gurus assumed I was lying. Neither was true. I was just working.

So I stopped waiting for someone else's protocol and started architecting my own — built for shift recovery, joint longevity, and the reality of a working professional's week. That system became BBF-Lab. Everything I teach, I stress-tested in the trenches first.
