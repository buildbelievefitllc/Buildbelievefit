Questions

Frequently
Asked.

The questions every high-demand human asks before signing the contract. If yours isn't here, the founder line is open.

Book A Discovery Call
  • Q · 01 What if my schedule completely breaks every week? +
    That's the entire design constraint. The protocol is built for humans whose week breaks — rotating shifts, red-eyes, on-call rotations. The architecture is schedule-agnostic by default; you don't earn flexibility, it's the floor.
  • Q · 02 Is the in-person component required for Architect & Sovereign? +
    Yes — these tiers exist because of the in-person clinical sessions. If you're remote, you belong in Gateway or on a custom remote-only contract. We don't dilute the tier.
  • Q · 03 How is BBF different from a normal personal trainer? +
    A trainer prescribes workouts. A Performance Architect engineers the habit system, biomechanical protocol, nutrition math, and recovery cadence as a single operating system. The workout is one output — not the product.
  • Q · 04 My youth athlete has had a previous injury. Are you set up for that? +
    Yes. The Youth Athlete tier is OT-informed with pediatric liability shielding and return-to-load protocols. We coordinate with their treating provider — we don't replace one.
  • Q · 05 What's the difference between Architect Hybrid and Sovereign? +
    Architect is one clinical session per week + full app stack. Sovereign is two clinical sessions, the AI Audio Meal Scanner, Virtual Chef, and a priority founder line with sub-3-hour response. Same standard. Deeper access.
  • Q · 06 Is there a refund policy? +
    All flat-fee 12-week protocols carry a 2-week sovereignty window. If, after the first two weeks of executing the protocol, you don't feel the architecture matches your demand profile — we refund the remainder, no contest.
  • Q · 07 Do you train women specifically? +
    The protocol is sex-aware, not sex-segregated. Hormonal and cycle considerations are integrated into the nutrition math and recovery cadence for every female client. Roughly 40% of our roster is women.
